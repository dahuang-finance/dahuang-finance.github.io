I am an Assistant Professor of Finance at Northeastern University’s D’Amore-McKim School of Business.

I am an empirical researcher interested in the role of institutional investors in the economy, with a focus on mutual funds, passive investing, and exchange-traded funds. I also study questions at the intersection of finance and taxation.

To reach me, please email da.huang@northeastern.edu.
